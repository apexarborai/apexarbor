About Us

An Affirma Capital
Portfolio Company

Apex Arbor Holding Limited is a globally-oriented investment group headquartered in Singapore and a subsidiary of Affirma Capital — one of the leading private equity investors across emerging markets, formerly Standard Chartered Private Equity. Affirma's investment committee members bring an average of 14 years of tenure with the firm, having invested over USD 5.8 billion across 99 companies since 2002.

Built on Affirma Capital's institutional governance framework — regulated by the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) — our structure combines the rigor of established private equity practices with a focus on technology-enabled investment strategies. We deploy capital with disciplined deal structuring, strong minority protections, and an intense focus on value creation.
